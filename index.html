About Us

We are a group of students who have created Kawsay- the perfect platform for all your COVID essentials.

Objective

At Kawsay meaning "good life" we attempt to provide everyone with as much a good life as we can in the midst of this pandemic.
Kawsay aims to provide a platform that connects low-income schools, hospitals, and neighborhoods with donors that can provide them with the necessary supplies to overcome this pandemic.

How to help or receive help?

The pandemic has shaken the globe and drastically changed our lives in a matter of seconds. Kawsay aims to help underfunded schools and low income neighborhoods by connecting them with donors!

↳Become a Donor: Thank you for taking the first steps to help out in our initiative! You should first sign up for an account. Then fill out the form and you will then be connected with places that require donations and you can direct donations to your place of choice.

↳Need Donations: To request a donation, business and schools have to be registered by law. First sign up for an account and log in. Then fill out the form and you will then be connected with a donor near your location very soon.
